The Back Country, Our Home.

Since the beginning, man has had a distinct fascination and connection with the outdoors. Once rapid development and urbanization came about, man began to lose touch with nature, and soon forgot about his old home completely. Now, many children do not have a means to experience earth's beautiful lands, and go much of their young lives without seeing it. We hope to ensure that every child has an opportunity to explore its majesty.
